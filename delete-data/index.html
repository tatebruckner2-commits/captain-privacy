Delete Your Data

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

This page explains how to request that your data be deleted from Captain Team Management, the team management app operated by Captain Team Management LLC. You can request deletion of some specific data without closing your account, or you can request full account deletion.

Option 1 — Delete Specific Data Without Closing Your Account

You can delete or clear most of your personal information directly inside the Captain app, without contacting us:

  • Profile information — Open the Captain app, go to Settings → Profile, and edit or clear your name, profile photo, phone number, jersey number, position, date of birth, or emergency contact.
  • Photos you uploaded — In the Photos tab on your team page, tap a photo you uploaded and choose Delete.
  • Saved locations — In the calendar event editor, you can remove saved locations you created.
  • Events, announcements, and stats you created (coaches only) — Coaches can delete any event, announcement, or stat entry they created from inside the app.

If you would like to delete a specific category of data that is not self-serve in the app (for example, all messages you have sent, all RSVP records, or all carpool data), email us at the address below and tell us what you want removed. We process these requests within a reasonable period, typically within 30 days.

Email us to delete specific data

Option 2 — Delete Your Entire Account

If you want to permanently delete your Captain account and all associated personal data, email us from the email address linked to your account. We will reply to confirm deletion within a reasonable period, typically within 30 days.

Email us to delete my account

What Gets Deleted

When we process a full account deletion request, we permanently remove or anonymize the following data tied to your account:

  • Your profile (name, email, phone number, profile photo, jersey number, position, date of birth, emergency contact)
  • Messages you have sent and any media you have uploaded (photos, files)
  • RSVPs, carpool offers, and stat entries you have created
  • Push notification tokens and device records
  • Authentication records and session history

What May Be Retained, and For How Long

In limited cases, we may retain some information after deletion where required for legitimate business or legal reasons:

  • Subscription and purchase records — required by Apple, Google, and our payment processor (RevenueCat) for tax, accounting, and chargeback purposes. Retained for as long as required by applicable financial regulations (typically up to 7 years).
  • Anonymized or aggregated data — data that no longer identifies you may be retained indefinitely for product analytics and reliability.
  • Records required by law — information we are legally required to keep, such as in response to a court order, retained only for the period required.
  • Backup copies — encrypted backups may contain your data for up to 30 days after deletion before they are overwritten on rotation.

Special Cases

If You Are a Coach or Team Owner

If you are the owner of a team, deleting your account will not automatically delete the team or its data unless you are the only remaining coach. We will work with you during the deletion process to ensure team continuity for other members or to delete the team entirely if that is your preference.

If You Are a Guardian

Guardians may also request deletion of their linked player profiles. Email us with the player's name and team and we will remove the linked records.

Account Deletion Is Permanent

Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use Captain again.
