Delete Your Account

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

How to Delete Your Account

You can request permanent deletion of your Captain Team Management account and all associated personal data by emailing us from the email address linked to your account.

Email us to delete my account

To confirm we are deleting the right account, please send your request from the email address you use to sign in to Captain. We will reply to confirm deletion within a reasonable period (typically within 30 days).

What Gets Deleted

When we process a deletion request, we permanently remove or anonymize the following data tied to your account:

  • Your profile (name, email, phone number, profile photo, jersey number, position, date of birth, emergency contact)
  • Messages you have sent and any media you have uploaded (photos, files)
  • RSVPs, carpool offers, and stat entries you have created
  • Push notification tokens and device records
  • Authentication records and session history

What May Be Retained

In limited cases, we may retain some information after deletion where required for legitimate business or legal reasons. This may include:

  • Subscription and purchase records — required by Apple, Google, and our payment processor (RevenueCat) for tax, accounting, and chargeback purposes.
  • Anonymized or aggregated data — data that no longer identifies you may be retained for product analytics and reliability.
  • Records required by law — information we are legally required to keep, such as in response to a court order.

If You Are a Coach or Team Owner

If you are the owner of a team, deleting your account will not automatically delete the team or its data unless you are the only remaining coach. We will work with you during the deletion process to ensure team continuity for other members or to delete the team entirely if that is your preference.

If You Are a Guardian

Guardians may also request deletion of their linked player profiles. Email us with the player's name and team and we will remove the linked records.

Account Deletion Is Permanent

Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use Captain again.
